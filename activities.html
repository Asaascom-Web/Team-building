Lift The Bucket

DESCRIPTION

Lift a bucket with full of water to pour into an empty bucket in 10 feet distance. Each participant only can hold the string to lift the bucket up and complete the task.

OBJECTIVE

Building a strong bonding with your team members, to work towards a same goal together.

Enhance effective communication skill among teammates to speed up the working process.

Manage to use limited resources to build and achieve some goals.

Lead a team in order to speed up the process and win the activities.

Bondspire - Team Building

Feedback Session

DESCRIPTION

The Feedback Session is a structured activity where team members come together to share constructive feedback with each other in a supportive environment. Participants reflect on the team-building activities they’ve engaged in, focusing on their individual and group experiences. The goal is to provide insights on performance, communication, teamwork, and areas for improvement, while reinforcing positive behaviors and outcomes. This session emphasizes open communication, active listening, and mutual respect.

OBJECTIVE

Foster a culture of continuous improvement and growth within the team.

Encourage individuals to reflect on their contributions and areas for development.

Build trust through transparent and honest communication.

Help the team identify strengths and opportunities for better collaboration.

Final Challenge Divide To Bridge

DESCRIPTION

In the final challenge, individual classes will build a portion of a bridge separately and can only communicate through their leaders with the other tribes. In the end when all portions of the bridge is brought together it must somehow FIT. A final test is to run a TOY remote control car on the bridge and the integrity and structure of the bridge must hold.

OBJECTIVE

Working with limited resources and thus having cost saving mindset but never lack on quality.

Planning for success and managing difficult situations and difficult people in the process.

Working in different departments or division but having the ONE tribe success mindset of a collective GOAL and DIRECTION.

Stop taking things for granted and being thankful for what we have and received as it can be taken away from us.
