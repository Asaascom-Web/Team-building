Sugeh Hill Exclusive Eco Resort

A Nature-Inspired Getaway for Adventury

Perched on the highlands of Kampung Janda Baik in Pahang, Sugeh Hill Exclusive Eco Resort offers a perfect retreat for families seeking a serene escape amidst the beauty of nature. This 8-acre haven is adorned with stunning landscapes, lush greenery, and an abundance of flora and fauna, making it an idyllic destination for relaxation and adventure.

The resort features a variety of multi-concept accommodation chalets designed to cater to different preferences, all at affordable prices. Whether you're strolling through the mesmerizing grounds or unwinding in the tranquility of the tropical rainforest, Sugeh Hill promises an unforgettable vacation experience that blends comfort with the wonders of nature.

Discover the ultimate satisfaction in a setting that rejuvenates the spirit and creates cherished memories for everyone.

Sugeh Hill Exclusive Eco Resort

Lot 495, Kampung Chemperuh, Kampung Janda Baik, 28750 Bentong, Pahang, Malaysia.
