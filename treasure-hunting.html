Treasure Hunting Adventure

Unleash your inner explorer with Bonspire's Treasure Hunting experience!

Activity Overview

Embark on an exciting adventure designed to test your problem-solving skills, teamwork, and strategic thinking. This immersive activity combines the thrill of discovery with the joy of collaboration, making it perfect for corporate team-building events, school groups, or anyone seeking a memorable day out.

What to Expect

1. Welcome & Briefing

  • Introduction to the Treasure Hunting adventure.
  • Detailed explanation of the rules and objectives.
  • Formation of teams with balanced skill sets.

2. The Hunt Begins

  • Teams receive their first clue or map.
  • Navigate through various checkpoints, each with unique puzzles, riddles, or physical challenges.
  • Decode messages and solve tasks to progress to the next stage.

3. Themed Challenges

Challenges can be tailored to your preferences or the event's theme. Examples include

  • Nature Hunt : Finding specific plants, rocks, or natural features.
  • Urban Adventure : Navigating landmarks in a city or town.
  • Historical Quest : Solving mysteries related to local history or folklore.

4. The Final Puzzle

  • A climactic, high-stakes challenge to locate the treasure.
  • Teams will need to combine their collective knowledge and skills for the ultimate reward.

5. Treasure Discovery & Celebration

  • The winning team claims the treasure and is awarded a prize
  • End with a celebration, group photos, and sharing of highlights.

Duration & Locations

The activity typically lasts 3 to 5 hours, depending on the group's size and the complexity of the challenges.

Choose from customizable settings such as :

  • Parks and nature reserves.
  • Beaches or islands.
  • Urban or historical landmarks.

Why Choose Treasure Hunting ?

  • Encourages teamwork and communication.
  • Boosts problem-solving and critical thinking.
  • Provides a mix of fun, adventure, and learning.
  • Ideal for building memorable experiences.
