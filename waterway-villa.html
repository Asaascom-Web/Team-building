Bondspire - Holiday Place (The Waterway Villa)

A Serene Escape in the Heart of Bukit Tinggi

Nestled within the tranquil tropical rainforest of Bukit Tinggi, Pahang, The Waterway Villa offers a serene retreat where nature’s beauty takes center stage. This peaceful sanctuary is the perfect escape from the city’s hustle and tropical heat, allowing guests to immerse themselves in the raw splendor of the Malaysian rainforest.

Our villas, meticulously crafted with classic traditional designs and inspired by the natural surroundings, provide a harmonious blend of comfort and elegance. Whether you’re seeking a family getaway, a romantic retreat, or a venue for corporate team-building, The Waterway Villa promises an experience that rejuvenates the body and soothes the soul.

Located just a 50-minute drive from Kuala Lumpur, this retreat is a convenient yet refreshing escape from urban life. Guests can enjoy a wide range of recreational and leisure activities amidst breathtaking views and invigorating fresh air.
